ABOUT

Olle Axelsson

Background

I am a software engineer and systems researcher based in Stockholm, Sweden. My work sits at the intersection of signal processing, distributed systems, and what I can only describe as the phenomenology of computation — how systems come to represent and process experience.

I studied computer science at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, graduating in 2018. After several years in infrastructure engineering, I moved into independent research.

From 2024, I was affiliated with ████████████████ as lead researcher on a project I am not currently able to discuss. This section will be updated when circumstances allow.

Signal architecture. Threshold systems. The boundary between noise and pattern.
